room 01 — the method

workflow

from spark to launch

A four-step method to reveal an aligned, singular and memorable identity.

01

let's meet

You share your brief, I gather your needs, goals and constraints to frame the collaboration.

02

i create & refine

I explore, sketch and build visual and editorial routes to give life to your universe.

03

we discuss

I present the concepts, we exchange live, you share feedback to polish every detail.

04

we launch

I finalise the deliverables and we launch your new communication together.

inclusive & responsible design

Accessibility, eco-design and performance are woven into each step so your story shines for everyone.

  • Contrasts audited, keyboard navigation and structured markup keep assistive technologies aligned.
  • Lightweight assets and progressive loading keep the experience fast and energy-conscious.
  • Inclusive tone and multilingual paths invite every audience to step into your universe.

room 03 — the artist

i'm anne

I'm Anne, the designer behind petitchardon. Since childhood, creation has been my playground: inventing worlds, telling stories, imagining adventures.

Today I channel that creativity into your narratives to turn them into remarkable, memorable visual identities.

My approach is direct, warm and action-oriented: I guide you towards a vision that feels true to you and impossible to ignore.

human-scale studio

You speak, I listen. Honest, fluid conversations turn your intuitions into tangible creative directions.

My background in psychology helps refine your needs. petit chardon becomes your teammate to get you noticed.

augmented creativity

Design, illustration and copywriting blend together to build vibrant, cohesive worlds — from logo to manifesto.

From identity to social media, coherence is your strength. Let's craft your signature language.

ready-to-launch assets

Brand kits, guidelines, templates and editorial content: leave with concrete deliverables ready to amplify your voice.

I stay by your side to run and grow your social channels over time.
