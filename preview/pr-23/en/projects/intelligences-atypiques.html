Intelligences Atypiques

Giving neurodiversity a face: a warm, structured identity built like a constellation, where every singularity has its place.

§ 01

the brief

A sensitive, rich subject. It needed an inclusive image — neither infantilising nor clinical — able to carry a nuanced message across social feeds and print alike.

§ 02

the process

A constellation metaphor structures the whole system: linked nodes, non-linear paths, a calm and legible two-ink palette. The social strategy turns that language into reusable templates.

§ 03

the pull

A coherent, recognisable presence that is easy to keep alive over time. The inclusive tone is written into the graphic system itself.
