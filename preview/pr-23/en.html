N° 02 — Process

workflow

from spark to launch

A four-step method to reveal an aligned, singular and memorable identity.

01

let's meet

You share your brief, I gather your needs, goals and constraints to frame the collaboration.

02

i create & refine

I explore, sketch and build visual and editorial routes to give life to your universe.

03

we discuss

I present the concepts, we exchange live, you share feedback to polish every detail.

04

we launch

I finalise the deliverables and we launch your new communication together.

inclusive & responsible design

Accessibility, eco-design and performance are woven into each step so your story shines for everyone.

  • Contrasts audited, keyboard navigation and structured markup keep assistive technologies aligned.
  • Lightweight assets and progressive loading keep the experience fast and energy-conscious.
  • Inclusive tone and multilingual paths invite every audience to step into your universe.
